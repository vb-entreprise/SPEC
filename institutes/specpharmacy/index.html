Sardar Patel College of Pharmacy

Sardar Patel College of Pharmacy, established in 2007, is one of the best Pharmacy institutes in Gujarat. The institute has been established with a view to promote excellence in pharmacy education and to prepare budding pharmacists to meet the challenges in the global era of pharmaceutical industries.

Located in the lush green environment on outskirts of Vallabhvidyanagar, the college contributes towards development of skill through practical experiences and development of vision among students by interacting with experts from pharmaceutical industries and education field.

About Sardar Patel College of Pharmacy

Sardar Patel College of Pharmacy, established in 2007, is one of the best Pharmacy institutes in Gujarat.Sardar Patel College of Pharmacy is affiliated with Gujarat Technological University (GTU), Ahmadabad and Approved by Pharmacy Council of India (PCI), New Delhi and Certified with ISO 21001:2018.

📚 Sanctioned Intake

  • • B.Pharm: 100 seats
  • • M.Pharm (Pharmaceutics): 15 seats
  • • M.Pharm (Pharmacology): 15 seats
  • • M.Pharm (Quality Assurance): 6 seats

🏛️ Accreditation & Affiliations

  • • GTU Affiliated
  • • PCI Approved
  • • ISO 21001:2018 Certified
  • • Established: 2007

🏗️ Infrastructure & Environment

Sardar Patel College of Pharmacy provides incomparable infrastructure which fulfils the norms of PCI and other statutory bodies. Located in an area totally free from pollution and congestion, it provides a suitable environment for sound concentration for study.

🔬 Facilities & Laboratories

The college has a sophisticated analytical laboratory to conduct analytical studies for therapeutic drug monitoring. Facilities include:

• Well ventilated spacious audio visual classrooms
• Equipped laboratories & machine room
• Analytical laboratories & animal house
• Seminar hall with LCD facility
• Medicinal garden with 50+ plant species
• Museum, boys & girls rooms

🎓 Learning Environment

Interactive learning environment with industry visits, workshops, seminars, and lectures. Well maintained medicinal garden with more than fifty species of plants for research.

💼 Placement Support

The placement cell collaborates with leading pharma industries and hospitals to provide employment opportunities. All final year B.Pharm and M.Pharm students enroll in placement activities.

🌟 Extracurricular Activities

Extracurricular activities are conducted for holistic development of students. The Extra-curricular Club aims at harnessing and exploring the talent of students apart from academic ability, focusing on all-around development.
